About GEU ACM


GEU ACM is a student-run organization that aims to promote the development of students in the field of computer science. We aim to provide a platform for students to learn and grow in their field of interest. We also aim to provide a platform for students to interact with each other and share their knowledge.

About GEU


Graphic Era Deemed to be University(GEU), formerly known as Graphic Era Institute of Technology, is one of the leading universities in developing India as it has become a popular university in a concise span of time. It has also been ranked 64th by the NIRF in 2021 rankings. The university offers Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Doctoral programs in streams like Engineering, Management, Computer Application, Humanities & Social Sciences, Commerce, Life Sciences, Hotel management, and Hospitality. Among these the most popular courses are B.Tech, B.Sc, and B.E.

GEU ACM Organisation

GEU ACM student chapter is run by an elite body of professionals who inspire and guide our students towards a glorifying future.
