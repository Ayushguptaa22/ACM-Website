GEU ACM APP

Download the ACM App to get the latest updates about GEU ACM.

GEU ACM brings to you the first ever app of ACM Student Chapter with numerous features. Keeping you notified of all the latest updates is its main motive. The list of upcoming events will be displayed in the app so that you don't miss out on your opportunities. Connectivity is yet another feature of this app which makes it different from the others. The contacts of all the members of the Core Committee, as well as the mentors, have been provided so that your questions do not go unanswered. You can clarify your doubts any time you want and that too with just a single click.
